Jeko – A fan made MTG set

Welcome ! I created this website to share a personal project I’ve been working on the past few months. This set has been made for draft sessions. Enjoy 🙂 !

This set works around new abilities, each of them have it’s specific colors and synergies in the set :

  • Curse : Put a Curse counter on target opponent.

Create a new ressource to use on the right timing in order to enhance the spells you cast and activate specific abilties, with a good strategy, you will take an advantage over your opponents !

  • Guard : Activate this ability at the end of your draw step and only once each turn. When you do, this creature gains defender until end of turn.

Protect your board, your lifepoint and activate this ability to control the game. Choose carefully when to use this ability as it doesn’t allow you to attack !

  • Prayer : Lose 2 life then add one mana of that permanent’s color.

Prayer is certainly a great way to ramp through out the game, use you permanents to get the right amount of mana, but watch out for your lifepoint, as it comes with a painful cost !

  • Last Breath : Activate this ability when this creature dies and only once.

Your creatures represent a huge threat to your opponent as they carry an ability that you can choose to activate when they die. It’s on you to attack and defend to get the most value out of them !

  • Tribal : This whole set is composed of a lot of types that you can find in multiple colors : Soldier, Zombie, Wizard, Human, Giant, Horror, Beast. As you draft, think of the synergies you can create through a tribal deck ! Also Changeling creatures will help you to complete your strategy.
  • And a lot more : Enchantements, auras, menace, vigilance, scry, all these features get a whole package to create a deck around them. Draft the cards and give them a shot ! You will not be disappointed 😉
